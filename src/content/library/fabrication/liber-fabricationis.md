# LIBER FABRICATIONIS

## The Book of Construction

**Repository Version 2.2 — Constitutionally Established**

### Purpose

Liber Fabricationis preserves the second era of Project Aurea: the physical and digital realization of Romanisti.

The Constitutional Library defines the institution.

Liber Fabricationis translates that institution into architecture, interface, behavior, imagery, components, and code.

Everything beginning with *Tabula Articuli* belongs to this book because it marks the transition from writing the institution to designing the institution.

The constitution governs.

The blueprint executes.
