# Liber Editorialis

## Constitutional Foundation (Locked)

Purpose:
The Editorial Manual implements the principles established by the Codex.

Editorial Mission:
Romanisti is an editorial institution dedicated to exploring the enduring relationship between Rome, AS Roma, and the Romanisti.

Rome is the center. Everything else is understood in relation to it.

Editorial Test:
Will this help someone understand Rome, AS Roma, and the Romanisti more deeply tomorrow than they do today?

Editorial Law I:
Understanding Before Publication.

Guiding Maxim:
Romanisti does not publish to explain today's events. It publishes to preserve tomorrow's understanding.
