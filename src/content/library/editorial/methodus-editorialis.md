# Methodus Editorialis

## Section I — Before We Write (Locked)

1. Observe
2. Understand Immediate Context
3. Seek Historical Continuity
4. View Through the Roman Lens
5. View Through the Institutional Lens
6. Consider the Romanisti
7. Ask the Editorial Question
8. Research
9. Reflect
10. Publish

Events begin the process.
Understanding completes it.
Only then does Romanisti write.
